About Us

Hello, we are the King family. We started Clean A Blind in 1997 after a frustrating attempt at trying to hand clean our own blinds.  We told ourselves “there had a be a better way” to handle this laborious task. This led us to do some research and we discovered a blind cleaning machine that uses ultrasonic technology.  We saw a demonstration and were so amazed at how well the machine cleaned the blinds, that we decided to form the business.  Over the years, we have served many happy customers in the Triad region with our blind and window cleaning services. What is Ultrasonic Cleaning?

Ultrasonic cleaning technology involves a stainless-steel tank, warm soapy water, and high frequency sound waves.  Sound waves create vibrations that travel through the water to form tiny bubbles. When a blind or shade is immersed into the water, these powerful bubbles repeatedly implode upon the surface to remove dirt, dust, and grease particles. The bubbles are microscopic in size which enables the blind to be thoroughly cleaned between the slats and the mechanisms inside the headrail as well as the cords and ladders.

Ultrasonic cleaning does not use toxic chemicals and is the most effective method for cleaning blinds and shades.  Many window covering manufacturers recommend this method of cleaning including Hunter Douglas and Graber.
