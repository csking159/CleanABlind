Services We Offer

Pick up & Delivery

When we arrive at your home or business, the blinds are removed and each one is numbered to assure it goes back in its original window. The blinds are transported to our facility where they are professionally cleaned and sanitized. We return the same day to re-hang the blinds. 

*10-15 blind minimum depending on Triad location

Drop-off

Save 25% by bringing your blinds to us and receive them back the same day.  

Onsite Cleaning

This service is offered for select businesses.  A minimum of 50 blinds is required and the facility must have access to electricity and a water source.
